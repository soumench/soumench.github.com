A fable is a short tale in which animals or inanimate objects are used to teach a moral lesson. 
Let’s check out the poem “Fable”. Ralph Waldo Emerson, an American poet, composed this poem.           
This poem gives us a message that everything, every being, living or non-living, huge or tiny,  exists for a reason in this world.

English Phrasal Verbs

Some particles of Phrasal Verbs

Up
usually tells us the following:
(i) to increase something,
(ii) to move something upward,
(iii) to move something closer to your eyes,
(iv) to boost something,
(v) to add something,
(vi) to do something positive,
(vii) to prepare for something,
(viii) to show intensity


Phrasal Verb Meaning
get up to rise from bed
made up invented, not true
look up to to admire
back up to give support
mock up to make a model of something
mix up to confuse
pick up to choose

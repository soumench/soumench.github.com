English Phrasal Verbs

Some particles of Phrasal Verbs

Off:
OFF usually tells us the following so that we may guess the meaning of the Phrasal verbs that use "off" with them.
(i) to stop something,
(ii) to loose contact with something,
(iii) to delay something,
(iv) to cancel something
(v) to be anxious over something

Phrasal Verb Meaning
Write off to cancel
call off to cancel, to withdraw
put off to evade, to delay
lay off to suspend ot terminate from work
live off depend as a source of income or support
see off to bid farewell, to say good bye

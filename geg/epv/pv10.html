10. Separable Verbs

(B) With some phrasal verbs, the preposition can move away from the verb, while with others it cannot.

Example: Break down:

The car broke down in the jungle. ( Preposition cannot be moved.)

Example: Keep up

Keep it up. ( "up" can be moved away from the verb "keep".

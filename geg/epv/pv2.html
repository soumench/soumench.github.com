English Phrasal Verbs

Nature of Phrasal verbs

1. Native English speakers usually use Phrasal verbs in spoken and informal English.
2. They also use some phrasal Verbs frequently in written formal English too (e.g. "look for").
3. The same Phrasal Verb can have more than one meaning in different contexts.
4. Usually mastering Phrasal Verbs is a headache for non-English speakers.

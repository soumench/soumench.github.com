English Phrasal Verbs

Nature of Phrasal verbs

1. Native English speakers use Phrasal verbs in spoken and informal English.
2. Some phrasal verbs are used in written formal English.
3. The same phrasal verb can have more than one meaning in different contexts.
4. Usually it is frustrating and a difficult topic to learn topic for non-English speakers.

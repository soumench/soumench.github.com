2. Why phrasals are confusing

Suppose a student want to translate "My friend didn't turn up." into Bengali, using a dictionary. that gives —

turn = ঘোরা
up = উঁচুতে

Then the student tries to join together those Bengnali words, and the result is a disaster. The English seems to be saying some nonsense .

However the true meaning of that English sentence is "আমার বন্ধু উপস্থিত হয় নি।".

The mistake came because we first searched "turn" in the dictionary, and afterwards we searched "up".

The way to avoid falling in that trap is looking for "turn up" in the dictionary. A good dictionary tells you that "turn up" means "arrive".

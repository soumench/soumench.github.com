English Phrasal Verbs

Some particles of Phrasal Verbs

Down
usually tells us the following:
(i) to decrease something,
(ii) to move something downward,
(iii) to reduce something,
(iv) to negate something


Phrasal Verb Meaning
fall down to be unsuccessful, fail, drop
cut down reduced in scope or length
break down cease to function
let down to disappoint
narrow down to remove less important options

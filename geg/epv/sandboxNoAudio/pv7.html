7. Online dictionaries that give good Bengali translations of phrasals.

There are some online dictionaries available that may or may not help a student in obtaining the actual / contextual meaning of a phrasal verb who learns English as a second language.

Google Translate is one of the popular resources. But unfortunately it is not cent percent failsafe as of now. You might get unexpected / weird result.

In my personal experience, I have seen some third party Bengali to English Dictionaries on the internet. But to be honest, I am not completely satisfied due to their embedded adwares, trackers, security certificate mismatch, excessive user tracking etc.

On the Android platform, from Google play store, I tested some Bengali to English Dictionary apps that have some usability but again you cannot not rely on them 100%. Also they might display awkward advertisments during their usage.

A conventional reputed paper Dictionary is a better option here. But not all can afford that in a poor country due to lack of money and scarcity of storage space.

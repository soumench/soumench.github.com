7. Some common Phrasal verbs

  1. look for → to search
  2. bear with → to tolerate
  3. carry on → to continue
  4. go on → to continue
  5. pick on → to treat badly or unfairly
  6. bear on → to influence, affect
  7. hold on → to wait
  8. hold back → to prevent an action
  9. keep on → to continue
  10. come of → to happen as a result,to be descended from
  11. get away → to escape
  12. get over → to recover from something
  13. get through → to overcome, to endure, to finish
  14. look around → to search for something
  15. hold back → to hesitate to act or speak
  16. write off → to cancel
  17. call off → to cancel, to withdraw
  18. put off → to hold, to delay
  19. lay off → to suspend ot terminate from work
  20. live off → depend as a source of income or support
  21. see off → to bid farewell, to say good bye
  22. ask in → to request
  23. check in → to register at a hotel, airport or hospital
  24. break in → to forcibly enter
  25. get in → to enter
  26. give in → to surrender
  27. rush in → enter quickly
  28. trade in → to exchange something
  29. set in → to begin
  30. stay in → to not go out
  31. bring out → to publish
  32. carry out → to execute
  33. fall out → to quarrel
  34. figure out → discover the solution
  35. roll out → to introduce, to include, to launch
  36. hang out → socialize
  37. pass out → to faint
  38. phase out → to remove gradually
  39. put out → to extinguish
  40. hand out → to distribute
  41. make out → to understand
  42. run out → to exhaust
  43. stand out → to become noticeable
  44. work out → to exercise
  45. bring up → to mention/ to rear
  46. call up → to remember
  47. get up → to rise from bed
  48. give up → to abandon
  49. make up → invented, not true
  50. look up to → to admire
  51. back up → to give support
  52. mock up → to make a model of something
  53. mix up → to confuse
  54. pick up → to choose
  55. fall down → to be unsuccessful, fail, drop
  56. cut down → reduced in scope or length
  57. break down → to stop working
  58. lay down → to sacrifice
  59. let down → to disappoint
  60. look down upon → to hate
  61. narrow down → to reduce in size or volume
  62. put down → to suppress
  63. pull down → to demolish
  64. do away with → to abolish
  65. get away with → to escape
  66. go through → to read minutely
  67. break into → to enter forcibly
  68. look upon → to consider
  69. tell upon → to affect
  70. get along / get on / get by → to manage / to survive
  71. get over → to recover from some unhappy or shocking experience
  72. run over → to hit with something or someone a car and drive over them

7. Online dictionaries that give good Bengali translations of phrasals.

There are some online dictionaries available that may or may not help a student in obtaining the actual / contextual meaning of a phrasal verb who learns English as a second language.

Google Translate is one of the popular resources. But unfortunately it is not cent percent failsafe now. You might get unexpected / weird result.

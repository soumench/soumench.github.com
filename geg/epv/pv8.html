English Phrasal Verbs

Some particles of Phrasal Verbs

Out:
OUT usually tells us the following so that we may guess the meaning of the Phrasal verbs that use "Out" with them.
(i) indicates absence,
(ii) to move outside,
(iii) to enlarge, to stretch
(iv) to resist something
(v) to search that is not obtained

Phrasal Verb Meaning
figure out discover the solution
roll out to introduce, to include, to launch
hang out socialize
phase out to remove gradually
hand out to distribute
stand out to become noticeable

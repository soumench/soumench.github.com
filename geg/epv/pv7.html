English Phrasal Verbs

Some particles of Phrasal Verbs

In
usually tells us the following:
(i) indicates a position,
(ii) to put or store something or some ideas inside something,
(iii) to invite, add or join,
(iv) to damage or injure something,
(v) to obstruct something


Phrasal Verb Meaning
ask in to request
check in to register at a hotel, airport or hospital
break in to forcibly enter
rush in enter quickly
trade in to exchange something
stay in to not go out
